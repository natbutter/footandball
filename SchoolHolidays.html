School Holidays

A brief summary of school holiday details.

School Holiday sessions are a promotional opportunity at the Academy. Often, we hope to be able to offer these sessions at a substantial discount to the general public.

These sessions are designed to offer insight into how our Academy functions, how we encourage or discipline the players, as well as educate them on football.

Holiday sessions will always include the three components of a session i.e., warm up, core message, and 1v1s. They will tend towards a broader theme due to the limited time available (as opposed to a 10-week semester).

Players will get to experience what our Academy has to offer them as well understand what we consider to be the most important aspects of football training.
