Privacy

At Foot and Ball Academy, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of our customers' personal information. We understand the importance of your privacy and are committed to complying with the Australian Privacy Principles set out in the Privacy Act 1988.

This privacy statement outlines how we collect, use, disclose, store, and protect your personal information, including any sensitive information, such as health or financial information. By providing us with your personal information, you consent to us collecting, using, disclosing, and storing your information in accordance with this statement.

Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information from our customers in a variety of ways, including:

  • Information provided by you through our website, registration forms, email, phone or in-person enquiries or meetings.
  • Information obtained through third parties with your consent, such as other soccer academies or schools that may refer you to us.
  • Information collected automatically through the use of our website or social media channels, such as IP addresses, device information or cookies.
  • The personal information we collect may include your name, contact details, date of birth, medical information, payment information and any other information necessary for us to provide you with our services.

Use and Disclosure of Perosnal Information

We use your personal information for the primary purpose for which it was collected, such as providing soccer coaching services, processing payments, sending you newsletters or promotional material, or communicating with you about our services. We may also use your personal information for secondary purposes that are related to our primary purpose, such as improving our services or conducting research.

We will not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent, except in circumstances where we are required or authorised by law, or where the disclosure is necessary for us to provide our services to you, such as sharing your information with third-party providers for payment processing or medical emergencies.

Storage and Secuirty of Personal Information

We take reasonable steps to ensure the security of your personal information, including implementing physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect against unauthorised access, modification, disclosure, or misuse. We also ensure that any third-party providers who handle your personal information comply with our privacy standards.

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Once your personal information is no longer needed, we will take reasonable steps to destroy or de-identify it securely.

Access and Correction of Personal Information

You have the right to access and correct your personal information held by us, subject to certain exceptions provided by law. Changes to this Privacy Statement

We may update this privacy statement from time to time to reflect changes in our privacy practices or legal requirements. The updated privacy statement will be posted on our website, and we encourage you to review it regularly.
