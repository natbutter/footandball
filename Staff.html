Head Coach and Founder

Blurb My background and pathway into soccer coaching is rather unorthodox. Well-travelled and educated, I believe my life experiences outside of football will contribute the most to my continued growth as an Academy coach. Joining the industry at a later stage in life, I have reflected carefully on my motivations for doing this, and want to ensure everyone that my main goal is to provide insight into the parts of coaching I now consider important, and never received in my youth. Playing History (2000 – 2011) Representative football (now NPL) from U/13’s. U/13’s – U/15’s – Hawkesbury City FC. U/16’s – Mount Druitt Rangers (half season) |Granville Rage (now Western Rage). U/18’s – Granville Rage (half season) | Macarthur Rams (half season). (2005) Member of travelling squad with Macarthur Rams to England. Training sessions at Cobham (Chelsea), and Melwood (Liverpool). Friendly non-league matches against Staines, Rochdale, Reading, etc. (2010) Six-week holiday visit to England. Training with semi-professional club in Southwest London. (2011) Gap year in England. Reserve match experience with semi-professional clubs in Southwest London. (2011) Day trial with EFL League 2 club (outcome: unsuccessful). (2000 – 2005) private coaching (Coverer-style curriculum). Ball skills, turns, passing, shooting, plyometrics, etc. Teacher History (2007 – 2010) Learn to swim instructor. Former AUST Swim qualifications. 1.5 – 16-year-olds. (2013-2016) University tutorial tutor. During post graduate studies, took undergraduate tutorials. Basic class preparation and delivery for a semester. (2022 – current) Kids soccer-football coach.
