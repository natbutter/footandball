Head Coach and Founder

My background and pathway into soccer coaching is rather unorthodox. Well-travelled and educated, I believe my life experiences outside of football will contribute as much as my player experiences to coaching. Joining the industry at a later stage in life, I have reflected carefully on my motivations for doing this, and want to ensure everyone that my main goal is to provide a coaching environment I now realise is important, and never received in my youth. e.g. improvment over winning.

U/13’s – U/15’s – Hawkesbury City FC.

U/16’s – Mount Druitt Rangers (half season) |Granville Rage (now Western Rage).

U/18’s – Granville Rage (half season) | Macarthur Rams (half season).

(2005) Member of travelling squad with Macarthur Rams to England. Training sessions at Cobham (Chelsea), and Melwood (Liverpool). Friendly non-league matches against Staines, Rochdale, Reading, etc.

(2000 – 2005) private coaching (Coverer-style curriculum). Ball skills, turns, passing, shooting, plyometrics, etc.
