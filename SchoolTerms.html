School Term Programs

Our core programs run according to the school terms. 10-week ‘curriculums’ designed to afford us the time to build towards better long-term player development. These programs give us the opportunity to get to know the player and better identify their strengths and weaknesses with the ball.

A typical curriculum will cover core topics plus combinations of each. E.g., dribbling, ball mastery, shooting, etc. Followed by dribbling AND ball mastery, dribbling AND shooting, etc.

All classes are limited to 6 enrolees or players. (The odd trialist may also attend, see trialist guidelines for more information). This ensures maximum attention can be provided by the coach to each player, whilst numbers are enough to ensure healthy social interactions, we design meaningful game-based exercises, and most importantly exciting 1 on 1 duels!

At this early stage of our business lifecycle, we offer the school-term programs to players aged between 5 and 12 years old. The sessions are a general mix of skill level, until we achieve the necessary numbers to begin offering different skill level sessions.

There is a small choice in sessions, running across two nights per week for Term 2 of 2023 (24th April – 30th June 2023). These days and times include:

Monday afternoon-evenings

  • 3.45pm - 4.45pm: 5-,6-,and 7-year-olds.
  • 5pm - 6pm: 8-,9-, and 10-year-olds.
  • 6.15pm - 7.15pm: 11-, and 12-year-olds.

Wednesday afternoon-evenings

  • 3.45pm - 4.45pm: 5-,6-,and 7-year-olds.
  • 5pm - 6pm: 8-,9-, and 10-year-olds.
  • 6.15pm - 7.15pm: 11-, and 12-year-olds.

Pricing and Player Commitments

All term programs have a minimum commitment of 1 session per week for that term i.e., 10 sessions.

If a player wants to join after the term has started, no worries! It is still a minimum commitment of 1 session per week for the remainder of weeks in that school term.

Payments are upfront at the beginning of term priced at: $25 per player per session or $250 upfront for the term. Or the remainder of the weeks as calculated from the players arrival during the term.

Players are to select a minimum of one time slot per week (from the available times listed above) according to their age group. Ages are determined based on the players age as of December 2023.

If a player wishes to do 2 sessions per week there is a $5 discount for the second session. Pricing then becomes: $25 for the first session and $20 for the second through the 10-week program. That equals an upfront payment of $450. Similar calculations as above if the player arrives during the term.

Siblings

Siblings also receive a $5 discount. That is, the first sibling is $25 per session for the term, and all subsequent siblings are $20 per term program.

Joining Fees and Uniform Fees

None! At Foot and Ball Academy we believe our no joining fee and no uniform fee is a financial advantage in this market space. We pride ourselves on having no additional fees beyond payment of the players direct footballing education. We strive to provide affordable access to our services as reasonably possible. Player improvement is truly at the foundation of Foot and Ball Academy.
