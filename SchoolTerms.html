School Term Programs

Our core programs run according to the school terms. 10-week ‘curriculums’ designed to afford us the time to build towards better long-term player development. These programs give us the opportunity to get to know the player and better identify their strengths and weaknesses with the ball.

A typical term curriculum has approximately a 65/45 split. 60-70% of our 10-week term sessions are spent working on 1v1 related exercises, ensuring the indvidual player is on the ball for the maximum amount of time. E.g., dribbling, ball mastery, defending, tricks and turns, as well as space utilisation when attacking. The other 30-40% of our allocated term sessions focus on passing and all it’s aspects. This latter component of the cirriculum has a 2 v 1’s and 3 v 2’s flavour to it.

All classes are limited to 5 enrolees or players. (The odd trialist may also attend, see trialist guidelines for more information). This ensures maximum attention can be provided by the coach to each player, whilst numbers are enough to ensure healthy social interactions, we design meaningful game-based exercises, and most importantly exciting 1 on 1 duels!

At this early stage of our business lifecycle, we offer the school-term programs to player age groups between U6’s (minimum 5) and U12’s. The sessions are a general mix of skill level, until we achieve the necessary numbers to begin offering different skill level sessions.

Pricing and Player Commitments

Payments are upfront at the beginning of term priced at: $26 per player per session or $260 upfront for the term. Or the remainder of the weeks as calculated from the players arrival during the term.

All term programs have a minimum commitment of 1 session per week for that term i.e., 10 sessions.

If a player wants to join after the term has started, no worries! It is still a minimum commitment of 1 session per week for the remainder of weeks in that school term.

Players are to select a minimum of one time slot per week according to their age group. Ages are determined based on the players age as of December EOY.

If a player wishes to do 2 sessions per week there is a $6 discount for the second session. Pricing then becomes: $26 for the first session and $20 for the second through the 10-week program. That equals an upfront payment of $460. Similar calculations as above if the player arrives during the term.

Siblings

Siblings also receive a $6 discount. That is, the first sibling is $26 per session for the term, and all subsequent siblings are $20 per term program.

Joining Fees and Uniform Fees

None! At Foot and Ball Academy we believe our no joining fee and no uniform fee is a financial advantage in this market space. We pride ourselves on having no additional fees beyond payment of the players direct footballing education. We strive to provide affordable access to our services as reasonably possible. Player improvement is truly at the foundation of Foot and Ball Academy.
